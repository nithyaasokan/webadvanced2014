Zombie Survival Plan

The Zombies are coming. What do you do?

This app is the answer to our most feared question. What do I do when the Zombie Apocalypse hits? Write in your location, hit submit and receive a master Zombie Survival Plan. From where to hide and where to find supplies to where zombies are likely to hit first and how to reach your family fast, this app is the ultimate guide to surviving.

That is, of course, if you are in New York. If not, good luck.
