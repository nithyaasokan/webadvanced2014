Instagram Color Cloud

  • Input : Hashtag
  • Output : Collage of Instagram photos
  • Audience : General / Tourists
  • Data : Instagram

People can enter a hashtag or a keyword into an input box. This will pull up all instagram posts relating to that keyword or using that hashtag. The data will be displayed as a collage of photos on a world map categorised according to their geographical location.
Choosing a theme could help narrow down the experience of this app. For example, for a theme of ‘Tourism’, the keyword/hashtag could comprise of words or names associated with various attraction sights around the world. The photos carrying a particular hashtag would be displayed as a world map that shows how many photos are clicked in various parts of the world.
