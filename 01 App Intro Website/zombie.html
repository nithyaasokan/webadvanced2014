Zombie Survival Route

  • Input : Location
  • Output : Series of Infographics
  • Audience : Zombie Enthusiasts
  • Data : Google Maps, Places, MTA service

People can input their address and the Survival Route throws up a survival plan in case of a Zombie Apocalypse. It would use data such as Google Places, MTA train schedules, distances from Google Maps, weather data of the location to determine the best plan to collect resources, find a good hideout spot, find the best time & route to get there and how long till the apocalypse hits you.
This will need setting up of certain parameters of what the apocalypse would entail. A sequence of events that is pre-determined. The output will be in terms of info graphics for each aspect/element of the survival plan that can be downloaded as a one-page PDF.
